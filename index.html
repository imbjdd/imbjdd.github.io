About Me

I started playing World of Warcraft on a private server when I was 7, and got into programming at 10. I finished my baccalauréat at 16, did a preparatory class at Louis-le-Grand, and later graduated from Sorbonne University. Those early experiences sparked my love for problem-solving and technology, which eventually led me to become a Software Engineer.

I’m passionate about programming, mathematics, and tackling complex problems. I also enjoy playing chess: send me a message if you want to play! Beyond coding, I’m deeply interested in AI Safety and d/acc, as I believe technology has the potential to create one of the greatest positive impacts on the world, and I want to contribute to it.
